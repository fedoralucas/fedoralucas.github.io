poça consulting

www.digitalclient.pt

Digital Client is Poça Consulting's platform that helps local businesses manage operations and communication in one place. It brings together online scheduling, a branded mobile app, analytics and integrated marketing tools to keep clients close and the day-to-day organised.

I designed a conversion-focused landing page to introduce Digital Client and drive trials and enquiries.

• Clear hero with value proposition, product visuals and primary CTA

• Benefit sections for Management and Communication, each supported by imagery and microcopy

• Short explainer videos, feature highlights and simple charts to convey results

• Social proof/testimonials and a contact form for leads

• Responsive layout prepared for desktop, tablet and mobile

I needed to communicate a broad feature set without overwhelming visitors and keep the tech credible yet approachable for small business owners. The page also had to be fast, accessible and SEO-friendly while preserving a clean brand look.

To meet these goals, the page uses tight messaging, clear hierarchy and robust implementation:

• One-line value statement plus scannable subheads to explain “what it is” and “why it matters” in seconds

• Split narrative (Management vs Communication) to group features and reduce cognitive load

• Dual CTAs (free trial / contact) placed at the hero and reinforced mid-page and footer

• Trust signals (logos/testimonials) and concise feature grids with icons for quick reading

• Performance and SEO care: optimised media, semantic HTML, descriptive meta and accessible contrast and tap targets
