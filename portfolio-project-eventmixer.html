EVENTMIXER

eventmixer

Eventmixer is a digital platform that helps people capture and share the best moments of concerts, trips and social gatherings. The brand combines the energy of live events with a clean, modern aesthetic that works well in interfaces. Its voice is friendly and enthusiastic, designed to feel accessible to both casual users and content creators.

Below is a clear summary of the scope and deliverables:

• Branding: logo system, colour palette, typography, layout rules and a full brand guidelines book

• Product (UX/UI): mobile and web flows — onboarding/login, “New Event” creation, templates gallery, video editor with timeline and publishing screens.

• Goal: one visual language that scales from identity to interface

BRANDING:

• Capture the idea of “mixing” and celebration without becoming noisy.

• Create a flexible symbol that works in small and large sizes, light and dark backgrounds

• Build a palette that feels lively but stays readable in UI contexts


APP (UX/UI):

• Make video-editing flows simple for non-experts

• Keep consistency between mobile and desktop while adapting to each platform

• Balance vibrant brand colours with accessibility and clear hierarchy

BRANDING:

• Concept built around modular, rounded bars — a dynamic mark that suggests mixing, rhythm and exclamation

• Warm-to-cool palette (orange/red → violet/purple → teal) plus neutral black/white for versatility

• Clean geometric sans typography and generous spacing for a contemporary feel

• Brand guidelines detailing construction grid, clear space, minimum sizes, colour/mono versions and misuse examples


APP (UX/UI):

• Mobile-first flows with clear CTAs and straightforward steps: Create Event → Pick Template → Edit → Export/Share

• Card-based templates gallery and a simplified timeline editor with focused controls

• UI kit and component library (buttons, inputs, chips, cards) to ensure consistency and speed up delivery

• Consistent use of rounded shapes and brand colours, with attention to contrast and legibility on light/dark surfaces

• Responsive web screens mirroring the mobile experience for continuity across devices
