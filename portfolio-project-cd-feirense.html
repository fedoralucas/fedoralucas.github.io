clube desportivo feirense

Clube Desportivo Feirense has a volleyball department of the historic club from Santa Maria da Feira, Portugal. Communication focuses on the team's identity, community spirit and season-long performance, using the club's blue-and-white visuals to deliver clear, energetic updates to fans.

Below is a concise summary of the volleyball social content produced:

• Matchday posts announcing fixtures across age groups

• Full-time posts reporting results shortly after matches

• Coach highlights and announcements

• Congratulatory posts for athletes (birthdays, milestones, awards)

The volleyball context required consistency and speed across multiple content types:

• Keep a strong Feirense identity while making each post type instantly recognisable

• Adapt layouts to variable data (opponents, dates, competitions, player photos, sponsors)

• Ensure readability on mobile and quick turnaround on game days

• Balance bold typography with imagery without clutter

Here's how I structured a system tailored to the volleyball department:

• Modular template kit with clear hierarchy (e.g., “JOGOS” headline), shield lockups and a sponsor strip

• Distinct layouts per type: fixture grids, result cards with clear score panel, portrait-led frames for coaches and birthdays (including player name and number)

• Club colours, textured backgrounds and a subtle season marker for continuity across posts

• Export presets and organised master files to speed up weekly production and keep the feed coherent
