SOCIAL MEDIA CONTENT

Dr. Eddy Costa is a medical doctor specialising in aesthetic medicine. His brand is focused on trust, professionalism and education, providing patients with reliable treatments and clear information about procedures and results.

I was asked to design a series of social media posts to strengthen the doctor's online presence:

• Educational posts explaining common treatments such as microneedling, fillers and botox

• “Before and After” visuals to demonstrate results

• Content addressing myths and common concerns in aesthetic medicine

• A visual identity adapted for Instagram, consistent with the doctor's professional image

The challenge was to translate complex medical topics into content that was easy to understand, engaging, and visually appealing. It was important to combine credibility and clarity while keeping a professional and trustworthy tone.

To achieve this, I developed a clean and informative visual style:

• A palette with soft colours to reflect calmness and professionalism

• Use of real photos and mockups to increase trust and relatability

• Layouts with clear hierarchy, balancing medical information and design

• Content structured for Instagram, optimised for engagement and readability

• Posts that educate patients while reinforcing the doctor’s credibility and brand
